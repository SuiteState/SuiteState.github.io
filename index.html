How we work

Three commitments that come from being on the receiving end of bad IT services for years.

01

Listening over selling

We start by understanding how your team actually works. Most "Odoo problems" are workflow problems pretending to be technical problems — and the reverse is also common.

02

Fair pricing

Day rates that reflect the actual work, not the size of your company. Open-source modules stay free. Paid work is scoped, fixed, and finished.

03

Compatibility first

Every module is built to coexist with native Odoo and other third-party modules. No invasive overrides, no version lock-in, no walls around your data.
